Welcome to XIAOMIAUS

Smile · Design · Simplicity

The name “Xiaomi” here is inspired by the Chinese expression “笑眯眯” (xiao mimi), which describes a person smiling warmly and kindly. It represents friendliness, positivity, and a sense of calm simplicity — values we believe can shape better design and better living.

XIAOMIAUS is a concept space built around the idea of “small beauty and big warmth” — embracing minimal design, creativity, and mindful living. It’s about enjoying the small details that make life feel balanced and meaningful.
